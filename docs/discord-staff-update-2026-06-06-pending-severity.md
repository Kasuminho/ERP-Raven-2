PT

Atualizacao exclusiva da Staff

A Central de Pendencias da Staff agora calcula a gravidade das tarefas pela idade real da pendencia.

Novas regras:

- Review de leilao: Medium com 3 dias, High com 5 dias.
- Entrega de drop de leilao: Medium com 10 horas, High com 1 dia.
- Codex pendente: Medium com 15 dias, High com 25 dias.
- Codex com retry/quebrou: Medium com 20 dias, High com 30 dias.
- Interesse fechado aguardando entrega: Medium com 10 horas, High com 1 dia.
- Progresso aguardando validacao: Medium com 2 dias, High com 3 dias.
- Evento aberto aguardando presenca/finalizacao: Medium com 10 horas, High com 20 horas.
- Item Request aguardando gestao: Medium com 2 dias, High com 5 dias.

Tambem ajustamos Item Request para aparecer como tarefa acionavel na Central, nao apenas como contador.

EN

Staff-only update

The Staff Pending Center now calculates task severity based on the real age of each pending item.

New rules:

- Auction review: Medium after 3 days, High after 5 days.
- Auction drop delivery: Medium after 10 hours, High after 1 day.
- Pending codex: Medium after 15 days, High after 25 days.
- Codex retry/broken: Medium after 20 days, High after 30 days.
- Closed interest waiting for delivery: Medium after 10 hours, High after 1 day.
- Progress waiting for validation: Medium after 2 days, High after 3 days.
- Open event waiting for attendance/finalization: Medium after 10 hours, High after 20 hours.
- Item Request waiting for management: Medium after 2 days, High after 5 days.

Item Requests now also appear as actionable tasks in the Staff Pending Center, not only as a counter.
