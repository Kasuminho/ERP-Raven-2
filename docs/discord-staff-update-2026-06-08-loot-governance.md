PT

Atualizacao exclusiva da Staff

Interesses agora possuem votacao antes da entrega.

Fluxo novo:

- OPEN: players ainda podem declarar interesse.
- VOTING: declaracao fechada e Staff vota em quem deve receber.
- READY_FOR_DELIVERY: alguem atingiu 3 votos e a entrega foi liberada.
- DELIVERED: Staff enviou o item e registrou a prova.

Regras:

- Staff/Admin pode votar em 1 interessado por rodada.
- O voto pode ser alterado enquanto ninguem atingiu 3 votos.
- Com 3 votos, o sistema trava o vencedor e libera a entrega apenas para ele.
- A entrega exige print/prova.
- Se houver empate, a Staff pode gerar desempate.
- O desempate cria nova rodada apenas com os empatados.
- Votos, desempate e entrega geram audit log.

Leiloes agora tambem passam por confirmacao da Staff.

Fluxo novo:

- Ao finalizar, todo leilao com bid valido vai para PENDING_REVIEW.
- Staff confirma o candidato vencedor.
- Se o candidato nao puder receber o item, Staff invalida o bid com motivo.
- O DKP travado desse player e liberado.
- O ranking recalcula e o proximo colocado passa a ser o candidato.
- Ao aprovar, o DKP do vencedor e consumido e os demais locks sao liberados.

Avisos de skills em interesses:

- Skills Heroicas/Epicas e Lendarias continuam com post individual.
- Skills mais fracas abertas em massa viram um unico card no Discord para reduzir spam.

EN

Staff-only update

Interest posts now require Staff voting before delivery.

New flow:

- OPEN: players can still declare interest.
- VOTING: declarations are closed and Staff votes on who should receive the item.
- READY_FOR_DELIVERY: someone reached 3 votes and delivery is unlocked.
- DELIVERED: Staff sent the item and registered proof.

Rules:

- Staff/Admin can vote for 1 interested player per round.
- Votes can be changed until someone reaches 3 votes.
- At 3 votes, the system locks the winner and enables delivery only for that player.
- Delivery requires proof screenshot.
- If there is a tie, Staff can generate a tie-break.
- Tie-break starts a new round with only the tied candidates.
- Votes, tie-breaks, and deliveries generate audit logs.

Auctions now also require Staff confirmation.

New flow:

- When finalized, every auction with a valid bid moves to PENDING_REVIEW.
- Staff confirms the winning candidate.
- If the candidate cannot receive the item, Staff invalidates the bid with a reason.
- That player's locked DKP is released.
- Ranking recalculates and the next candidate becomes selected.
- When approved, winner DKP is consumed and other locks are released.

Interest skill announcements:

- Heroic/Epic and Legendary skills still get individual posts.
- Lower-tier skills opened in bulk are grouped into one Discord card to reduce spam.
