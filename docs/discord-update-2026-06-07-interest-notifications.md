PT

Atualizacao no fluxo de interesses

Agora as postagens de interesse abertas pela Staff tambem sao publicadas no canal de interesses do Discord.

Quando a Staff registra entrega de interesse, o sistema tambem publica o registro no canal de drops entregues.

EN

Interest flow update

Interest posts opened by Staff are now also published in the Discord interest channel.

When Staff registers an interest delivery, the system also posts the delivery record in the delivered drops channel.

The flow is still managed through the website:

- Staff opens the interest post from the Items screen.
- Player declares interest on the website with 1 screenshot.
- Staff closes, reviews, and registers the delivery.
- Discord is only used for announcements and public history.

ES

Actualizacion del flujo de intereses

Las publicaciones de interes abiertas por Staff ahora tambien se publican en el canal de intereses de Discord.

Cuando Staff registra una entrega de interes, el sistema tambien publica el registro en el canal de drops entregados.

El flujo sigue siendo gestionado por el sitio:

- Staff abre el interes desde la pantalla de Items.
- El player declara interes en el sitio con 1 captura.
- Staff cierra, revisa y registra la entrega.
- Discord se usa solo para avisos e historial publico.
